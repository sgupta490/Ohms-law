Ohm's Law

PROCEDURE:

  1. 1. Set the value of resistance R1, R2 and R3 in KOhm.
  2. 2. Set DC voltage.
  3. 3. Voltmeter is placed parallel to resistor and ammeter in series with resistor.
  4. 4. Now click on current button to calculate the current.
  5. 5. Change the value of resistance and voltage again. Take another reading
  6. 6. Repeat step 2 to 6 for another set of resistance value.
